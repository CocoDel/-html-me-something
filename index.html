Cultural Family Trip to Perú

Animal Sanctuary

"There are so many places to visit using their eco friendly superhighway of the jungle - The Amazon River. We visited an animal sanctuary where you can get up close with most of their animals; from playful monkeys, to exotic birds, to anacondas!!"

Mystical, Historical, Spiritual Land of Perú

"Remains of the ancient cities of the Incas with well mapped out infrastructure are still accessible to tourists."

Food in Perú is an Art

"The people of Perú love their food. It is natural or as we like to say organic. There are 30+ variations of potatoes and hundreds of recipes for potatoes. Here we are enjoying al fresco restaurant by the Amazon River. The fish is caught that same day and it's prepared and served that same day. It can't get any fresher and delicious - yum yum "
